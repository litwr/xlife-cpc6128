`cpcconv' converts Commodore U00 and optionally S00 files to appropriate 
Amstrad CPC 8L and TXT files.

USAGE
    cpcconv file.U00 [file.S00]


`raw2cpc' converts a raw file produced by lifeconv to Amstrad CPC 8L format.

USAGE
    rawcpc rawfile cpcfile.8l

These utility are scripts using bash, cat, tr, sed, awk, dd.
